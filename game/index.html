The Introduction

BABABOOEY

"GAME" is a 4 player 2.5D futuristic co-op shooter game in which players take the roles of special forces soldiers fighting against aliens. Players must complete various tasks in levels in order to progress. The players can choose 1 of 4 roles on their team – medic, engineer, support or recon. Each role has their unique perks and abilities.
